What is Discord Ball Z ?

Discord Ball Z is a free text based RPG developed for the Discord platform inspired by the Dragon Ball Universe.

In this game you will be able to collect Dragon Ball characters, train them, build a team of your favorite characters and lead them to combat against powerful foes !

Each character is unique, meaning that each character has its own passive skills (such as leader and passive) and set of abilities.

Note

Please note that the game has been inspired by the mobile game Dokkan Battle but it is not a copy of it, meaning that you might not find all the characters you know from this game in Discord Ball Z.

What is my goal as a player ?

There is no goal defined, you decide of what you want to accomplish in the game.

Here is a list of non-exhaustive goals you might decide to have :

• Collect all the characters in the game.
• Being the richest tradesman in the game (Buying reselling, item farming, etc.)
• Having the strongest team in the game and grind as much Muscle Tower floors as possible.
• Being at the top of the ladders each season.

And much more ...
To be honest, there is no real goal, you are the only one who can decide what to do in the game, and there is a lot to do !
